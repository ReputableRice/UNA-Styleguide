Main Logo

UNA's logo comes across as a representation of qualities such as togetherness (or in other words, "una"), moderness, and recognizeability. These aspects are showcased through 2 main factors: colour and iconography. Our icon hosts a hotpink colour, declaring it's presence using vibrant, yet not overwhelming through sheer simplicity.

Additionally, the inverted design is a combination of two themes of imagery, holding hands (which represents "togetherness") and a bunny (which reflects friendliness)
